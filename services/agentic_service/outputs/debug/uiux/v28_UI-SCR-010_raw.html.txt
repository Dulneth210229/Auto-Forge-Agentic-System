This wireframe includes the following components:

1. Header: Navigation menu with links to different sections of the website.
2. Hero Section: Promotional section with a hero image, headline, and call-to-action button.
3. Products Grid: Grid container with product cards that include images, names, prices, and add-to-cart buttons.
4. Product Details: Detailed view of a single product with an image, name, price, and buy-now or add-to-cart buttons.
5. Cart Summary: Summary of the user's cart contents, including products, quantities, and total cost.
6. Order History: Table listing the user's past orders, including order dates, totals, and statuses.
7. Account Dashboard: Dashboard for the user's account, with links to their order history, address book, and password change options.
8. Footer: Copyright information at the bottom of the page.

Note that this is just a starting point, and you will need to customize the design and functionality to fit your specific requirements.
