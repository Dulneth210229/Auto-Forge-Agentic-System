This wireframe includes the following pages:

1. **Header**: A navigation bar with links to different sections of the website.
2. **Hero Section**: A hero section that showcases a product or promotion.
3. **Products Grid**: A grid view of products, each with an image, name, price, and "Add to Cart" button.
4. **Product Details**: A page that displays detailed information about a specific product, including an image, description, and "Add to Cart" button.
5. **Cart Summary**: A summary of the user's cart, showing the products they have added, along with a total price and a "Checkout" button.
6. **Checkout Form**: A form that allows users to enter their name, email, and address before placing an order.
7. **Order Confirmation**: A page that confirms the user's order has been placed successfully, displaying the order details and allowing them to view their order history.

This wireframe provides a complete high-fidelity e-commerce UI/UX design pack with navigation, cards/forms/tables where needed, realistic microcopy, clear actions, no blank pages, and a visible complete user flow diagram.
