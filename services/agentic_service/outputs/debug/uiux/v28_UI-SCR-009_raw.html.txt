This wireframe includes the following components:

* Header with navigation menu
* Hero section with call-to-action button
* Products grid with product cards and add-to-cart buttons
* Product details page with product information, add-to-cart button, and buy now button
* Cart summary page with cart contents, total, and checkout button
* Order history page with order table
* Account information page with form to update account information

Note that this is just a starting point, and you will need to customize the design to fit your specific requirements. Additionally, you may want to add more components or features depending on your specific use case.
