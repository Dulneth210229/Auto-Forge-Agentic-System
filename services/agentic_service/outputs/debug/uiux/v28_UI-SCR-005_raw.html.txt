This wireframe includes the following pages:

1. **Header**: A navigation bar with links to different sections of the website.
2. **Hero Section**: A hero section that welcomes users and encourages them to shop now.
3. **Product Grid**: A grid of products with images, names, prices, and add-to-cart buttons.
4. **Product Details**: A page that displays detailed information about a product, including an image, name, price, and description.
5. **Cart Summary**: A summary of the user's cart, including items and total cost.
6. **Checkout Form**: A form that allows users to enter their shipping and billing information to complete a purchase.
7. **Orders History**: A page that displays the user's order history, including dates, totals, and links to view order details.

Note: This is just one possible design for each page. You can customize the design to fit your specific needs and branding.
