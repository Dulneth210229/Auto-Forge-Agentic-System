This wireframe includes the following components:

* Header with navigation menu
* Hero section with hero image and call-to-action button
* Product grid with product images, names, prices, and add to cart buttons
* Product details page with product image, name, description, features, reviews, and add to cart button
* Cart summary page with table displaying products, quantities, and totals
* Checkout form page with form fields for customer information and place order button
* Order summary page with table displaying products, quantities, and totals

Note that this is just a starting point, and you will need to customize the design to fit your specific needs.
